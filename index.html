About
Me
Hello! I'm Stephan. I am a business owner, graphic designer, and burgeoning web developer. After spending 14 years in the wholesale screenprinting and embroidery industry building a successful business, I had a moment of intense introspective reflection and realized how very, very bored I was. So I did what any reasonable husband/father-of-three/self-employed individual would do in that situation; I put the business on auto-pilot and sought out a career in a field I had absolutely zero experience in! I signed up for a 6-month 'coding bootcamp' through Michigan State University and started learning full-stack web development. This is an ongoing journey that is only just beginning for me and I cannot wait to see where it goes! Also, perhaps, you may consider hiring me someday. Thanks!
