About
Me
Hello! I'm Stephan. I am a business owner,graphic designer, and burgeoning web developer. After spending 14 years building a successful business in the wholesale screenprinting and embroidery industry, I had a moment of intense introspective reflection and realized how very, very bored I was. So I did what any reasonable self-employed/husband/father-of-three would do in that situation; I put the business on auto-pilot and sought out a career in a field I had absolutely zero experience in! I signed up for a 6-month 'coding bootcamp' through Michigan State University and started learning full-stack web development. I was taking a risk diving head first into an industry I no first-hand experience with, but I immediately fell in love with coding and everything about it. This is an ongoing journey that I am only at the beginning of and I cannot wait to see where it goes! Also, perhaps, you may consider hiring me someday. Thanks!
